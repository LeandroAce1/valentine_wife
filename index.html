Ingrid, mi amor

Eight years of you has been the greatest gift of my life.

My love, every version of me has loved every version of you. Through laughs, long nights, dreams we built from nothing, and now the beautiful family we share — you have been my home. I still look at you the way I did eight years ago, except now I love you even deeper.

Thank you for choosing me, for believing in us, and for being the most amazing mother and partner. I don’t need a perfect life — I just need you.

Will you be my Valentine, today and always?
